Exploring the metaverse with Miguel

David Cash sits down with Grammy Award-winning artist Miguel.

By 

David Cash

Published 

March 12, 2025

SHARE

David Cash sits down with Grammy Award-winning artist Miguel and Hawa from Spatial.io to explore how new technology can bring together different creative fields. Miguel discusses his transition into roles beyond music, focusing on creative direction and entrepreneurship within Web3.

‍

‍

Miguel expresses his attraction to Web3, particularly the concept of decentralization, which resonates with his background of self-reliance taught by his father. He envisions that augmented reality will continue to develop and enhance life experiences, making them more accessible and engaging – even predicting a world where all the clothes we wear day-to-day will be digital.
