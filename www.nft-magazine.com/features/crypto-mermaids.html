Collect a Crypto Mermaid

An Accenture study found that 50% of women abandon tech careers by the age of 35. An estimated 75% of crypto holders are men. Crypto Mermaids have an answer.

The Crypto Mermaids address the knowledge gap between men and women through absurdly fun education where crypto, NFT and Web3 are our doorways into financial empowerment.  

Crypto, with its merger of technology and finance culture creates unique barriers to women and others that feel isolated from it.  Web3 offers the chance of creating a new financial system. Most of us don’t understand the existing financial system. The Crypto Mermaids want more people to understand and be empowered through technology and financial literacy. 

‍

The Crypto Mermaids don’t care if you ever buy crypto or not. We only care that you make this choice from a place of knowledge and empowerment.

Why now is the best time to learn about crypto

Designed as a gateway into financial empowerment and literacy for women, the Crypto Mermaids take advantage of popular curiosity about crypto to increase general literacy around money and personal finance.  With a cooler crypto market we have time to slow down and consider what it means to the average person. Something that is often overlooked in conversations about crypto is the fundamentally different technology that underlies its existence. There may be a lot of uncertainty about crypto but the Crypto Mermaids feel certain that it will be part of everyone’s future.

Why Gretchen started the Crypto Mermaids

Since becoming disillusioned with the sexist culture of silicon valley artist Gretchen Andrew has been using her increasingly visible art practice to increase popular understanding of new and important technologies.  In 2021 she formalized her internet activism and efforts in internet literacy into The Crypto Mermaids, an open community demystifying male-dominated knowledge topics in a fun, female, and approachable way. Gretchen’s heartbreaking journey leaving major tech companies emphasizes how sad it is to leave a place you love but don't feel you belong. Changing technology and the demographics of those who benefit from it have always been part of Gretchen's mission as an artist. Every crypto mermaid has their own story as to why this matters to them. 

  1. Gretchen entered silicon valley obsessed and excited. She even dressed up as the Mozilla Firefox for Halloween her junior year in college.
  2. Things didn't go as planned, as Gretchen spoke about the culture of silicon valley and her resulting decision to become an artist (Fast Company Homepage story)
  3. Gretchen started the Crypto Mermaids to change the culture around technology

How the Crypto Mermaids Support Web3 Empowerment Through Education 

  1. Beach Club Book Club: Monthly gatherings encouraging crypto education hosted by Monique Leong at homes and pools all around the world with a home base at Barbican Beach Club.
  2. Writing and promotion for the Crypto By the Pool book publication (under contract)
  3. Education in media through writer/editor positions at The Art Newspaper, NFT Magazine, Fad Magazine and others
  4. Production of guides, video & multi-media content: We bring new people into Web 3 by meeting them where they are
  5. Partners with non-crypto finance for workshops on traditional finance

About Crypto Mermaids

Crypto Mermaid originates from Gretchen Andrew’s Studio Team. Gretchen Andrew is best known for her playful hacks on major artworld and political institutions. Her fine art NFTs are in institutional collections and regularly feature in exhibitions with The NFT Gallery, Vertical Crypto, SuperRare and others. Gretchen Andrew’s Studio Team releases projects aimed at improving literacy and empowerment in male-dominated knowledge topics.
